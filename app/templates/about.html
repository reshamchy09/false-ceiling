Our Story

Building Beautiful Ceilings Since 2016

Dhangadhi False Ceiling was founded with a vision to transform spaces through innovative ceiling solutions. Starting as a small local business in Dhangadhi, we have grown to become one of Nepal's most trusted false ceiling specialists.

Our journey began with a simple belief: every space deserves a beautiful ceiling. Over the years, we've completed more than 500 projects across Kailali and surrounding districts, earning the trust of homeowners, businesses, and contractors alike.
